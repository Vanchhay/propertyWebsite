About Us

vKirirom Resort House

Our houses are situated amongst the pine trees and on top of the Kirirom Mountain. We aim to provide people from different walks of life a house that is surrounded by nature's gift. A place where it is peaceful, quite and away from the hustle and bustle of the busy city life.
