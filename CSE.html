About Us

vKirirom Vacation Home

Our houses are situated amongst the pine trees and on top of the Kirirom Mountain. We aim to provide people from different walks of life a house that is surrounded by nature's gift. A place where it is peaceful and quite and away from the hustle and bustle of the busy city life.

vKirirom Project

Our project is a new concept for people who loves nature and wants to escape the busy city life. Give us your house design and we build it for you.

What's so special ?

Kirirom is surrounded by beautiful greenery and fresh clean air. A very good place to invite your friends for a weekend of camping, pinic or even trekking. Kirirom is also home to some beautiful water falls, bird and other wildlife.
